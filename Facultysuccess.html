Faculty are vital employees in our institution, who can add and remove students from the system. This is a faculty page for an institution that allows faculty to generate examination papers. Faculty can add and remove questions from the question bank, which will be randomly selected for students to answer. Click on the 'add question' tab to add questions to the question bank. Faculty can choose to allocate time for examination, course code, course name, and the number of questions to display in the question paper. Each choice can have a significant impact on how students will approach the exam. It is important for instructors to be aware that there are many different ways they can design their exam questions.
